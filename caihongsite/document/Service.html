Public Engagement and Outreach

Bevrijdingsfestival Groningen 2026

Annual Liberation Festival · May 5, 2026 · Groningen, The Netherlands

Together with the Speech Lab Groningen team, I took part in the SpraakLab mobile laboratory at the Groningen Liberation Festival. We presented research methods in the language sciences to a broad public audience — sharing how we study speech, perception, and bilingualism with visitors of all ages.

Zpannend Zernike — National Science Weekend 2025

University of Groningen · October 2025 · Groningen, The Netherlands

At the Zpannend Zernike National Science Weekend, I engaged the public on linguistic variation through an interactive board game on the regional languages of the Netherlands. I also took part in articulatory phonetics demonstrations using ultrasound tongue imaging, interacting with diverse audiences — both adults and children.

Conference and Workshop Organization

11th Rencontres des Jeunes Chercheurs en Parole (RJCP 2025)

Co-organiser · November 2025 · Paris, France

I co-organised the 11th edition of the Rencontres des Jeunes Chercheurs en Parole (RJCP 2025), held in Paris under the auspices of l’Association Francophone de la Communication Parlée (AFCP). I was also the organiser and coordinator of the Eye-Tracking Workshop held at RJCP 2025, hosted by the Laboratoire de Linguistique Formelle (LLF).

26th Rencontres des Jeunes Chercheurs de l’ED 622

Co-organiser · 5–6 June 2023 · Paris, France

I co-organised the 26th edition of the Rencontres des Jeunes Chercheurs de l’ED 622 “Sciences du langage” on the theme Norme(s) et transgression(s) en Sciences du langage : contextes, pratiques et enjeux, jointly organised by Université Paris Cité and Université Sorbonne Nouvelle.
